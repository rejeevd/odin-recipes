Classic Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide flat pasta sheets with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Baked until golden and bubbly, it's a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions. With its savory flavors and satisfying texture, lasagna is a timeless favorite around the world.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2–3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or pork (or a mix)
  • 1 can (28 oz / 800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes, splash of red wine

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a 9x13 inch (23x33 cm) baking dish.
  3. Layer noodles, meat sauce, béchamel, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  4. Repeat layers (usually 3 total), finishing with béchamel and cheese on top.
